Pasta

Pasta dish

Description

Pasta in a garlic tomato sauce is the ultimate comfort meal. Made wtih pasta, chicken, spinach, and parmesan cheese, you can make this dish in under 30 minutes!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Chicken breast
  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Spinach
  • Heavy cream
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Prep Pasta: Boil water in a large pot and cook pasta al dente according to package instructions.
  2. Season both sides of the chicken with salt, garlic powder, Italian seasoning, paprika, and a generous pinch of salt and pepper.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large heavy duty pan over medium-high heat and cook chicken breasts 5 minutes per side
  4. To the same pan, add the butter and garlic and cook for 1 minute or until fragrant
  5. Add the tomato and spinach and cook 2-3 minutes or until the tomato and spinach are soft and wilted.
  6. Reduce the heat and add the heavy cream and parmesan cheese. Whisk until fully incorporated and creamy. Add a pinch of salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Drain pasta and slice chicken into thin slices or cubes and return to the same pan.
  8. Stir to combine and serve!
